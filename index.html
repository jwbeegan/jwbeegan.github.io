Hello, my name is Jonathan Beegan and I am a programmer striving to create interesting and innovative games. I graduated from Purdue University with Honors in the spring of 2021. I have been working on my own games since 2022, and released MechAvenger, a VR platformer shooter on Steam in January 2024.

I have also worked full-time as a PLC programmer for Hurco Companies Inc. since November 2021. In my time there, I have worked on complex, critical problems under tight deadlines. I have also acted as a liaison with international partners to fix production issues.

Working on my games has been my passion for the last 3 years. From the initial prototypes, to releasing finished products, I enjoy the entire process. I am always improving my skills with new programming languages, game making strategies and design techniques.

When I am not developing or playing games I enjoy spending time with my family and my pets, exercising and playing Magic: The Gathering. I am currently looking for new oppurtunities, so contact me if you would like to chat!
